OpenPitch Account Deletion

This page explains how to delete your OpenPitch account and what happens to your data when your account is deleted.

How to Delete Your Account in the App

  1. Open the OpenPitch app and sign in to your account.
  2. Tap Profile.
  3. Open your account settings from the Profile area.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Follow the confirmation prompts to permanently delete your account.

If you cannot access the app and need help with account deletion, contact us at support@openpitchapp.com.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, we delete or de-identify the personal data associated with your account, including your account profile and account credentials, except where retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or operational reasons.

What Data May Be Retained

We may retain limited information where necessary to:

  • comply with applicable law or legal obligations
  • protect against fraud or abuse
  • resolve disputes or enforce our agreements
  • maintain appropriate business and financial records where required

Retention Period

Account deletion requests are typically processed promptly. Any retained data is kept only for as long as necessary for the specific legal, security, or operational purpose requiring retention.
